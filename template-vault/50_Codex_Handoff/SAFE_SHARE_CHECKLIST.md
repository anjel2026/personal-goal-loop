# Safe Share Checklist

## Identity

- [ ] No real names.
- [ ] No email addresses.
- [ ] No phone numbers.
- [ ] No addresses.
- [ ] No employee, account, or record identifiers.

## Business

- [ ] No real organization, store, department, customer, or vendor names.
- [ ] No payroll, attendance, sales, or financial raw data.
- [ ] No internal policies or secrets.
- [ ] No raw files attached.

## Files

- [ ] No workbook originals.
- [ ] No PDF originals.
- [ ] No image originals.
- [ ] No screenshots with private information.
- [ ] No private local paths.

## Prompt Quality

- [ ] The requested outcome is clear.
- [ ] Inputs and outputs are described.
- [ ] Stop rules are included.
- [ ] Success criteria are testable.
- [ ] Public-ready improvements are separated from private context.

## Decision

- [ ] Safe to share.
- [ ] Needs more redaction.
- [ ] Keep private.
